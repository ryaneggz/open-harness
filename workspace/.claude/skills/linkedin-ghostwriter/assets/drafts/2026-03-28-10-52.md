Every agent I ship starts with this file. Zero boilerplate — just headers and dates:

```markdown
# Memory

## Decisions
## Preferences
## Daily Log
### 2026-03-28
```

No pre-filled examples. No "insert your context here."

🧠 Pre-filled templates train agents to parrot your words back. Blank sections let them develop 𝘵𝘩𝘦𝘪𝘳 𝘰𝘸𝘯 working memory.

📌 After 3 days, the agent's MEMORY.md reads like meeting notes you didn't have to take.
