🔧 The migration:
- Copied SOUL.md and MEMORY.md into the #OpenHarness workspace
- `make NAME=dev quickstart`
- Each agent got 𝘪𝘵𝘴 𝘰𝘸𝘯 container. Full permissions inside, zero access outside.

🧠 Before: shared host, crossed wires, 3 incidents/week.
After: isolated sandboxes, 54 commits, zero breakouts.
