My agent ran flawlessly in the #OpenHarness sandbox. Shipped it to a client's VPS.

😅 First crash: `/var/run/docker.sock` not found. Then missing `.env` vars. Then an API key with a trailing newline from `echo`.

🔧 What survived the move: `SOUL.md` and `MEMORY.md` — bind-mounted files, fully portable. What broke: every host assumption I never thought to test.

🧠 Your agent's 𝘪𝘥𝘦𝘯𝘵𝘪𝘵𝘺 is portable. Its 𝘦𝘯𝘷𝘪𝘳𝘰𝘯𝘮𝘦𝘯𝘵 isn't. Open Harness makes the first part easy — you still have to ship the second.
