A property manager in St. George told me she had 23 unanswered Guesty reviews. Some were 3 weeks old. 😅

I pointed an #OpenHarness agent at her Guesty API:

🔧 Heartbeat runs every 30 minutes
🔧 Pulls new reviews, reads past guest notes from the CRM
🔧 Drafts a 𝘱𝘦𝘳𝘴𝘰𝘯𝘢𝘭𝘪𝘻𝘦𝘥 response — not a template
🔧 Queues it for her approval before posting

First cycle cleared 8 reviews in under 4 minutes.

She didn't need a VA. She needed a HEARTBEAT.md with 6 lines in it.
