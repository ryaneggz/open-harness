ServiceTitan marks a job complete. My agent catches the webhook, pulls the line items, and creates the invoice in QuickBooks — before the tech gets back to the shop.

🧠 The pattern:
- ServiceTitan API → job status change
- Agent reads scope of work + materials from the dispatch
- QuickBooks invoice drafted, line items matched, ready for review

📌 One client was spending 45 min/day on manual invoice entry. That's gone now.

The agent runs inside an #OpenHarness sandbox with a HEARTBEAT.md that polls ServiceTitan every 120 seconds. Sandboxed. Logged. Disposable if anything goes sideways.
