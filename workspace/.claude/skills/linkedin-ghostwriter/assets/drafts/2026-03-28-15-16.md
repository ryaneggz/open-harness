The app was an internal tool. Three users. A shared password would've worked.

🧠 Agents optimize for the prompt, not the goal. Now I add acceptance criteria to every task in AGENTS.md:

✅ 𝐁𝐞𝐟𝐨𝐫𝐞: "Add auth"
✅ 𝐀𝐟𝐭𝐞𝐫: "Add auth — 3 internal users, shared secret via .env, no token refresh"

Two sentences. Saved me rolling back 47 files.
