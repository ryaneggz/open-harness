I woke up to 6 drafted LinkedIn posts this morning. Wrote zero of them. 😅

🔧 My `HEARTBEAT.md`:

```
HEARTBEAT_INTERVAL=1800

## Tasks
- Read style-guide.md for voice
- Pick next topic from queue.md
- Draft post (50-200 words)
- Save to drafts/
- Seed next topic
```

Every 30 minutes the agent wakes, checks this file, does the work, goes back to sleep. No cron jobs. No Zapier. One markdown checklist in an #OpenHarness sandbox.

Steal this. Swap "LinkedIn posts" for whatever your team does on repeat 👇
