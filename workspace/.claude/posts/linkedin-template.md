# LinkedIn Post Template

**Author:** [Ryan Eggleston](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ryan-eggleston)

---

## Structure

1. **Hook** (first line) -- make them stop scrolling. Ask a question, state a surprising fact, or challenge a common assumption.
2. **Context** (2-3 sentences) -- what you built/discovered/solved and why it matters.
3. **Value** (3-5 bullet points or a short narrative) -- what the reader will learn or gain.
4. **CTA** (last line) -- read the full post, star the repo, book a call, try the code.

## Format

- 50-200 words
- First person ("I", "we")
- Include relevant link (blog post URL, repo URL)
- End with hashtags on a separate line

## Example

[Hook line that grabs attention]

[2-3 sentences of context about what you built and why]

Here's what makes it different:

- [Bullet 1 -- key feature or insight]
- [Bullet 2 -- practical benefit]
- [Bullet 3 -- what they can do with it]

[CTA -- link to full post or repo]
