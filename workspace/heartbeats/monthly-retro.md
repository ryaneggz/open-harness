# Monthly Retrospective

Full strategy review on the 1st of each month. Evaluates whether the approach is working.

## Tasks

1. **Run the strategy-review skill** in full retrospective mode
2. **Benchmark comparison** — compare portfolio returns since inception against:
   - Pure All Weather (30% VTI / 40% TLT / 15% IEF / 7.5% GLD / 7.5% DBC)
   - SPY buy-and-hold
   - 60/40 traditional (60% SPY / 40% AGG)
3. **BTC cycle-out scorecard** — track cumulative Sharpe improvement from each BTC reduction
4. **Rolling Sharpe analysis** — are 30-day and 90-day Sharpe ratios improving?
5. **Position attribution** — biggest winners and losers by dollar and percentage
6. **13F alignment check** — is the portfolio still aligned with Bridgewater's latest macro thesis?
7. **Write "State of the Portfolio" entry to MEMORY.md** with:
   - Month-over-month return and Sharpe trend
   - Whether the 13F-informed tilt is outperforming pure All Weather
   - BTC cycle-out progress and impact
   - Biggest winners and losers
   - Recommendation: stay the course, or adjust the approach
8. **Update portfolio/scorecard.md** with latest report card
